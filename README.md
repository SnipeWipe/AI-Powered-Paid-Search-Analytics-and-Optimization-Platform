# AI-Powered Paid Search Analytics & Optimization Platform

## App Link - [Link](https://paid-search-optimization-platform-8khd8gtpunnzoqnsnqko5z.streamlit.app)
## Overview

The AI-Powered Paid Search Analytics & Optimization Platform is an end-to-end marketing analytics solution designed to improve customer acquisition performance through advanced analytics, machine learning, experimentation, optimization, and Generative AI.

The platform helps marketing teams optimize paid search investments by providing actionable insights across campaign performance, bidding strategies, budget allocation, attribution, experimentation, customer quality prediction, and AI-powered search visibility.

This project was inspired by enterprise-scale Paid Search Analytics functions used by leading organizations to drive acquisition efficiency and maximize marketing ROI.

---

## Business Problem

Marketing teams often struggle with:

- Inefficient budget allocation
- High customer acquisition costs
- Poor visibility into campaign performance
- Manual reporting processes
- Lack of explainability in machine learning models
- Difficulty measuring true channel contribution
- Limited ability to generate strategic recommendations at scale

This platform addresses these challenges through automation, machine learning, optimization algorithms, and GenAI-powered analytics.
